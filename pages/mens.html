Men's Clothing

Based in Tokyo, we have meticulously gathered an exquisite array of men's fashion from around the world. Our selection showcases renowned Japanese heavyweights like WTAPS, Needles, and Cav Empt, embodying the essence of Japanese style. You'll also find the laid-back West Coast vibe from brands like Brain Dead, Stussy, and General Admission, alongside the sleek minimalism of Scandinavian labels such as Our Legacy and Norse Projects. Furthermore, we offer cutting-edge European brands like Stone Island, celebrated for their technical fabrics and groundbreaking fashion innovations.
